In Your Feels Movies

Coach Carter

Great movie, great story. Really makes you feel something. Definitely a classic and a must watch.

Blind Side

One of my all time favorite movies. Definitely could watch this over and over and over!

Remember the Titans

This one really made me cry. The message in this movie is strong. Must watch just make sure you bring some tissues to wipe your tears.

Freedom Writers

What a great movie, WOW! I loved it so much.

Lean on Me

Morgan Freeman is one of my favorites. He does not miss! This movie is awesome.

Holes

A great family movie. Kids and adults can all enjoy this Disney film. Great message but also fun and easy to watch.

Love and Basketball

A must watch with your significant othetr. Break open a bottle of wine and enjoy this cinematic masterpiece.

Million Dollar Baby

Loved this movie but I definitely did not expect it to end the way it did. Lot of emotions were felt during this film. A must watch for sure!
